Introduction
GREEN offers finance to all in-store retail and online businesses against Point-of-Sale (POS) Machines or Payment Gateway proceeds. You can use this finance for expanding your business, opening another outlet, financing your inventory, fixed assets, debtors or any other operational expense that you may have in mind. You can repay this loan through easy monthly installments from proceeds of sales collected through your POS machine.
